Parcel Swift Logistics Analysis

Parcel Swift is a courier and parcel delivery company headquartered in the UK with 740 staff experiencing an increased rate of absenteeism in the past year, especially with front line delivery staff. This has led to delayed deliveries, customer complaints and rising overtime costs. Using PowerBI, an analysis was performed to understand the impact of varying factors on the reasons for staff absenteeism. Factors such as tenure, employee travel distance to work, social habits, education, if parents or not as well as what generation they belonged to were analyzed to understand the root causes of the business problem.

Novamed Sales and Inventory Management Analysis

Novamed is a leading pharmaceutical distributor facing challenges with optimizing sales, managing inventory efficiently and identifying key market opportunities. Utilizing data from the previous year, a Top/Bottom and Customer Analysis was performed using PowerBI to analyse sales metrics, customer demographics, buyer types and purchasing behaviours to gain insights into month-to-month trends, key revenue sources and top performing and underperforming drugs. These insights guided recommendations to support Novamed's strategic direction.
